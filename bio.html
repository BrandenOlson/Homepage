Bio

I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees in Applied Mathematics from the University of Colorado at Boulder, where I developed novel stochastic weather generators under the supervision of Will Kleiber. I moved to Seattle to pursue a Ph.D. at UW in September 2016.

Currently, I am a research associate for the Matsen Group at the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center where I am developing statistical methods for B Cell receptor sequence analysis.

I have internship experience as a data scientist for Paysa, analytics developer for Seagate, and software engineer for Spectra.
