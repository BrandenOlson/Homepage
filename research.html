Research

Broadly speaking, my interests lie in statistics, computer science, machine learning, and biology. More recently I've established an enthusiasm for problems in computational immunology.

Current areas of interest include:

  • B cell receptor (BCR) and T cell receptor (TCR) sequence analysis
  • Computational phylogenetics
  • Variational inference
  • Deep learning
