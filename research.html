Research

Broadly speaking, my interests lie at the interface of statistics, computer science, stochastic modeling, and machine learning. More recently I've established an enthusiasm for problems in computational immunology.

Current areas of interest include:

  • B cell receptor (BCR) sequence analysis
  • Computational phylogenetics
  • Latent variable models
  • Approximate Bayesian computation
